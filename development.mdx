---
title: 'Deleting Account'
description: 'Deleting account and all data'
---

<Info>
  **WARNING** Before proceeding, please note that deleting your account data is an irreversible action, and the data cannot be recovered.
</Info>

Once this action is performed, it cannot be undone, and the deleted YeikCar account will no longer exist in our system.

### Android/iOS

Step 1. Go to Settings App on the bottom.

Step 2. Go to the YeikCar profile.

Step 3. Select `Delete Account`
