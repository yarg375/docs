<Warning>
  **WARNING:** You can only import a maximum of 7 vehicles.
</Warning>




### Android/iOS

Step 1. Go to `Settings` App on the bottom.

Step 2. Go to `Manage Data`.

Step 3. Select from `Sources` **YeikCar Classic** and locate the previously exported database in the device directory.

Step 4. And finally, click the `Import` button.
